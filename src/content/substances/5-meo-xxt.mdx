5-MeO-xxT is not just one drug; 5-MeO-xxT is a group of drugs. Drugs in this group include 5-MeO-DMT, 5-MeO-DALT and 5-MeO-DET. Drugs within the same group can have very different dosages and very different effects.

## Effects
+ euphoria, stimulation
- unconsciousness, nausea, muscle tension, confusion, anxiety
~ visuals
