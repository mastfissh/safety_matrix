2C-x is not just one drug; 2C-x is a group of drugs. Drugs in this group include 2C-B, 2C-E and 2C-I. Drugs within the same group can have very different dosages and very different effects.

## Effects
+ euphoria, empathy, stimulation
- nausea, muscle tension, confusion, anxiety
~ visuals

## Oral Dosage of 2C-B
| Effect | Dose |
| --- | --- |
| Threshold | 2-5 mg |
| Light | 5-15 mg |
| Common | 15-25 mg |
| Strong | 25-50 mg |
| Heavy | 50+ mg |
