## Effects
+ euphoria, stimulation, increased sociability
- muscle tension, anxiety, insomnia
~ decreased appetite, increased heart rate, local anaesthesia

## Nasal Dosage of Cocaine
| Effect | Dose |
| --- | --- |
| Threshold | 5 mg |
| Light | 10-30 mg |
| Common | 30-60 mg |
| Strong | 60-90 mg |
| Heavy | 90+ mg |
